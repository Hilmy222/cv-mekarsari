About Us

Established since 1986, CV. MEKAR SARI is a corporate entity that specializes in providing services and operating as a contractor in the Contractor and Supplier sectors. Since its inception, the company has built a strong and credible reputation for delivering reliable and up-to-date solutions for supply and contracting needs in the Offshore and Marine services context. Through its dedication and ever-growing experience, CV. MEKAR SARI remains committed to delivering high-quality services and contributing positively to the advancement of this industry.

Our company’s growth is driven by the passion and professionalism of our team, ensuring that every project we undertake meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. Whether it’s contracting or supply, we ensure a seamless and effective service experience.

As the offshore and marine services industry continues to evolve, CV. MEKAR SARI remains committed to staying at the forefront of these advancements, continually improving and expanding our capabilities.
