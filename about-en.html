Established in 1986, CV. MEKAR SARI is a business entity specializing in providing services and operating as a contractor in the Contractor and Supplier sector. Since its inception, the company has built a strong and credible reputation for providing reliable and up-to-date solutions for supply and contract needs in the Offshore and Marine services context. Through dedication and growing experience, CV. MEKAR SARI remains committed to providing high-quality services and contributing positively to the advancement of this industry.

The growth of our company is driven by the passion and professionalism of our team, ensuring that every project we handle meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. Whether in contracts or supplies, we ensure a smooth and effective service experience.

As the offshore and marine services industry evolves, CV. MEKAR SARI remains committed to staying at the forefront of this progress, continually improving and expanding our capabilities.
